Ransomware Most Wanted - Actor Profiles

Officially wanted, indicted, or sanctioned ransomware principals and the supporting ecosystem selected for tracking. The first section covers operators, group leadership, and deployment affiliates (direct actors); the remaining sections cover function-specific support roles (access, laundering, hosting, facilitation). Photographs are drawn from official wanted pages where available and auto-load from the source; sanctions-only designations frequently carry no official photo. Confidence labels reflect the strength of public-source attribution. Not for operational use; verify against primary sources before action.
Compiled for analytical / briefing use. Sources: FBI Cyber's Most Wanted, U.S. Secret Service, U.S. State Dept Rewards for Justice, U.S. Treasury OFAC, UK NCA, Australia DFAT, EU Most Wanted (Operation Endgame / BKA), INTERPOL, U.S. DOJ indictments. Photographs remain the property of the issuing authorities and are shown for identification purposes.
